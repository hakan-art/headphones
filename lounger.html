The Lounger

The Lounger is your trans-sensory vessel to audio-hapticpleasure. Precision sculpted from luxe material, this high-tech pod is calibrated for comfort and inspired by the Japanese art of origami. Through its seamlessly integrated tablet, it delivers a rich and varied playlist of full-body experiences, with tracks updated monthly.

Produced in the Eu, this limited edition lounger is suitable for events, clubs, airlines, hotels, workplaces and indulgence-seeking individuals.

Available from February 2018
